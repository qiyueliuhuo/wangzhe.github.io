Daily words

1. Word

undermine

Definition
  1. to subvert or weaken insidiously or secretly. eg. trying to undermine his political rivals.
  2. to weaken or ruin by degrees.
More Examples
  1. She tried to undermine my authority by complaining about me to my boss.
  2. The events of the past year have undermined people's confidence in the government.
Make sentences

Setbacks in life sometimes undermine our confidence in future.

2. Word

navigate

Definition

intransitive verb

  1. to travel by water : SAIL. eg. navigated down to the mouth of the river.

transitive verb

  1. to sail over, on, or through. eg. able to navigate the deep ocean waters
  2. to make one's way over or through : TRAVERSE. eg. navigate the Internet via hypertext links connecting information-rich computers around the world.
More Examples
  1. For thousands of years, sailors navigated by the stars.
  2. How about if you drive and I navigate?
  3. He has had experience navigating airplanes through storms.
Make sentences

Now when people drive, they sometimes navigate by mobile phone.
